SDL_TextureModulate

An enumeration of the texture channel modulation used in SDL_RenderCopy().

Values

SDL_TEXTUREMODULATE_NONE

no modulation

SDL_TEXTUREMODULATE_COLOR

srcC = srcC * color

SDL_TEXTUREMODULATE_ALPHA

srcA = srcA * alpha
